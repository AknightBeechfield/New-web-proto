Anyone buying a cap labelled water-resistant deserves a straight answer to "how water-resistant?" Here's ours.

What the finish does

The B191R has a Non-PFAS, eco-friendly water-repellent finish. It resists light showers, brief splashes, and unexpected weather.

What it doesn't do

The fabric is breathable, which means the pores stay open. Under prolonged or heavy rain, water will eventually pass through both fabric and seams. The cap isn't seam-taped. It's not waterproof.

Why Non-PFAS matters

The treatment uses Non-PFAS chemistry, ahead of the France ban on PFAS-based treatments in textiles from 1 January 2026.

Composition: 100% Recycled Polyester (195gsm) with Non-PFAS water-resistant finish.

Care:

  • Machine wash at 30°C
  • Do not bleach
  • Low tumble dry
  • Cool iron
  • Do not dry clean
  • Avoid fabric softeners — they will reduce the water-resistant finish

GRS — Global Recycled Standard

The Global Recycled Standard is an international, voluntary standard that sets requirements for third-party certification of recycled content, chain of custody, social and environmental practices, and chemical restrictions.
