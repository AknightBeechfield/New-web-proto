How water-resistant it is

The Non-PFAS finish sheds light showers and brief splashes. It is not seam-taped and not waterproof — heavy or prolonged rain will get through.

Colour & washing

Slight colour variation between batches is normal. Wash darker shades separately for the first few washes, as some excess dye can release.

Caring for the finish

Avoid fabric softeners — they reduce water repellency. The finish softens gradually with washing and wear.

One-size fit

Unisex, 57–60 cm with an adjustable buckle. It suits most adults but isn't a guaranteed fit for every head.
