Precision in every journey. The QD540 Zürich Travel Backpack is engineered for the working week and the weekend after. Every pocket placed. Every essential to hand. Every day on time. Clamshell main compartment, padded 16" laptop sleeve and rear luggage strap. Cabin-compatible at 50 × 31 × 20 cm, in 100% GRS certified recycled polyester. Like clockwork, every day.

Cabin-baggage reality

At 50 × 31 × 20 cm the Zürich fits standard hand-luggage allowances on major carriers (IATA guideline is 55 × 40 × 22 cm). It is sized as primary hand luggage, not as a budget-airline free underseat bag. Always check your carrier's current dimensions before travel.

How the PU coating performs

The matte finish sheds light rain and wipes clean. It is water-resistant, not waterproof. Treat the bag as protected against weather, not submersion.

Composition: 100% Recycled 600D Polyester Matte PU coating · 100% Recycled 900D Polyester

Sponge clean only. Avoid solvents and harsh detergents — they break down the PU coating. Prolonged sun will degrade the finish over time.

Printing the QD540

The coated fabric is heat-sensitive, so press with care:

  • Protect the panel with a cushioned cover — polyester felt over Teflon — so the transfer edge doesn't imprint the bag.
  • On a flatbed press, raise the print area on a Teflon cushion to keep pressure off the zip.
  • Hold the transfer with heat-proof tape and let the rest of the bag hang clear of the platen.
  • Press around 15 seconds at 145°C, medium pressure. Works for both vinyl transfer and DTF.
