Product description

The Mini Essential Fashion Backpack keeps it simple: a compact shape, a clean front to brand and a colour range that goes from soft pastels to standout shades. Light enough to grab and go, roomy enough for the everyday carry, and built to be branded — the mini that earns its place in a giveaway, on campus, or off-duty.

What to expect
It's a real backpack, not a novelty

"Mini" is the shape, not a gimmick. At 23 × 35 × 12 cm it holds the daily essentials — phone, purse, keys, a few extras. Roomier than the name suggests.

Colour & batches

Shades are consistent batch to batch, but screens and lighting shift colour. Order a sample if an exact match matters for your brand.

Decorating it

The flat front panel takes print and embroidery cleanly. The TearAway® label lifts out so you can brand it as your own.

Keeping it clean

Polyester wipes down easily. Lighter shades show marks sooner — a damp cloth handles most of it.

Composition & care

Composition: 600D Polyester.
