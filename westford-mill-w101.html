Product description

Westford Mill's original Bag for Life, made for daily use and built for branding. A smooth, tightly woven 140gsm cotton with a 67 cm long handle drop, the W101 carries clean print results across all 48 shades. Bleached chlorine-free, finished with a cut-away label for easy rebranding, and packed for volume. Ready to go from box to press.

What to expect
Cotton, not canvas.
The W101 is a 140gsm shopper-weight cotton. Lightweight, easy to carry, easy to fold. For heavier weights, see the W201 (200gsm) or W801 (340gsm organic canvas).
Colour and washing.
Bleached chlorine-free with hydrogen peroxide. Pigment-dyed colours may show minor variation between dye lots. Order matched batches for large single-run jobs.
Decoration prep.
Pre-prepared for decoration, no pre-press cleaning required. For heat transfer, pre-press at 150°C for 15 seconds to flatten the surface and remove moisture.
Care reality.
Cold wash, low-temperature iron. Avoid heat over 150°C on decorated areas. Cotton softens with use, the bag will live in well over time.
